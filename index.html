Coal Mines Across the USA

About this map

This is a map showing the distribution of and Coal Mines across United States of America.

It was created using publicly available US Energy Information Administration (EIA) & US Geological Survey (USGS) data from 2016.

Map by Ritchie Katko

Created in Fall 2018 for MAP 673 Final Project.
